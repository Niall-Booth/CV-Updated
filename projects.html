Tic-Tac-Toe-GUI

This my Tic-Tac-Toe game where the user selects the square that they want to place their move on and then my program chooses either a random unoccupied space or if the user is a about to win it will place its move to block their path. Every turn the game will check to see if either player has won by checking over the board and seeing if three spaces in a line are filled by one player.
